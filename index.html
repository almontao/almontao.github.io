About Me

Linguist with experience in the fields of language that involve the Neuroscience and Artificial Intelligence.

As particular areas of interest and development are the understanding of the functioning of language in the brain (Neurolinguistics) and solving problems related to Natural Language Processing (NLP).

In addition to knowledge of programming languages such as Python, there is also experience using techniques such as Electroencephalography (EEG) and Eye Tracking.

Knowledge and practice in the editorial production process: revision, correction and publication.

What I Do

As a language professional who applies the scientific method to study the sounds of speech, grammatical structures and the multiple linguistic phenomena that occur in any language in the world, I have developed research skills that rely on areas such as Linguistics, Neuroscience, Statistics and Computer Science.

Data Processing

Experience in capturing massive data for later storage, pre-processing, analysis and interpretation.

Artificial Intelligence

Ability in the implementation of different Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI and Natural Language Processing algorithms.

Laboratory Attitudes

Trained in scientific research applying international biosafety protocols and bioethical principles.

Academic Production

Instructed in the academic production process, going through the different editorial stages: production, revision, correction and publication.
